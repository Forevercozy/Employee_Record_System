The employee management system aims to benefit our local managers, increase our local opportunities, and our economy by growing businesses. This will be done by encouraging the creation of more job opportunities, stabilizing the economy by having steady business growth, and improving business growth by helping our managers.

The Employee Record System is not a complex system. It has basic functionalities such as the CRUD functions, Database connectivity, and security. Since it is to keep records and nothing more, we chose not to complicate the system, and also because of the limited time given to complete the project.
